Our Curriculum

Our academic program is designed to be rigorous and comprehensive, preparing students for success in college and beyond. We follow a curriculum that emphasizes core subjects while offering a wide range of electives.

Programs Offered

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Focus on foundational skills in reading, writing, and mathematics.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Building on foundations with an introduction to science, social studies, and arts.

High School (Grades 9-12)

Specialized tracks in Science, Arts, and Commerce, with AP course options.
